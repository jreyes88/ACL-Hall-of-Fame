Previous Ceremonies

2016 Austin City Limits Hall of Fame

The 2016 Austin City Limits Hall of Fame took place October 12, 2016, at ACL's studio home, Austin’s ACL Live at The Moody Theater.

Inductees were B.B. King, Kris Kristofferson, Bonnie Raitt, and Dick Peterson.

Performers included Willie Nelson, Mavis Staples, Rodney Crowell, Gary Clark Jr., Billy Gibbons, B.B. King Band, Taj Mahal, and Eve Monsees.

Comedy super couple Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally served as emcees for the evening.

2015 Austin City Limits Hall of Fame

The 2015 Austin City Limits Hall of took place June 18, 2015 at ACL Live at The Moody Theater.

Inductees were Asleep at the Wheel, Loretta Lynn, Guy Clark, Townes Van Zandt, Flaco Jiménez, and ACL Crew - Season 1.

Performers included Vince Gill, Jason Isbell, Lyle Lovett, Patty Loveless, Gillian Welch, Laura Marling, David Hidalgo, Los Texmaniacs, JT Van Zandt, and more.

Hosted by Dwight Yoakam.

2014 Austin City Limits Hall of Fame

The first Austin City Limits Hall of Fame induction ceremony took place April 26, 2014 in KLRU's Studio 6A, the original home of Austin City Limits.

The ceremony featured the induction of Willie Nelson, Stevie Ray Vaughan & Double Trouble, Darrell Royal, and Bill Arhos.

Performers included Emmylou Harris, Lyle Lovett, Buddy Guy, Kenny Wayne Shepherd, Robert Randolph, Doyle Bramhall II, Mike Farris, and Lukas Nelson.

Hosted by Matthew McConaughey.

A second concert and induction ceremony was held on June 26, 2014. This event, titled ACL 40th Anniversary: A Benefit Concert for KLRU-TV, Austin PBS, took place at ACL Live at The Moody Theater.

The event featured the induction of Lloyd Maines.

Performers included Alabama Shakes, Doyle Bramhall II, Jeff Bridges, Gary Clark Jr., Sheryl Crow, Joe Ely, Grupo Fantasma, Robert Earl Keen, Kris Kristofferson, Lloyd Maines, Bonnie Raitt, and Jimmie Vaughan.

Hosted by Jeff Bridges and Sheryl Crow.
